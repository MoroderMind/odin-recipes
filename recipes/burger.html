Burger

Description

This classic simple burger recipe is perfect for a quick and delicious meal. With just a few basic ingredients, you can create a flavorful and satisfying burger that everyone will love

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 4 Hamburger buns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Form the patties into 4 equal portions
  2. Season both sides
  3. Preheat a pan
  4. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each size for a medium doneness
  5. Place each cooked patty on the bottom half of a bun
  6. Add ketchup above the burger
  7. Serve and enjoy!
