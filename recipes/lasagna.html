Lasagna

Description

This super simple lasagna recipe requires minimal ingredients and effort, making it perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. It's a straightforward approach to a classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 4 cups (32 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups (16 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (16 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef, drain fat, and add marinara sauce. Simmer.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Layer a 9x13 inch baking dish with sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers twice.
  6. Top with remaining sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
