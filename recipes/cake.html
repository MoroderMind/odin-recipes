Cake

Description

This super simple cake recipe is perfect for a quick and easy dessert. With just a few basic ingredients, you can whip up a delicious cake in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine flour, sugar and baking powder
  3. Add melted butter, eggs, vanilla extract, milk and a pinch of salt
  4. Mix well untill the batter is smooth and well combined
  5. Pour batter into a pan
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy!
